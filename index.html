Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the spark that ignites progress, transforming the impossible into reality and propelling humanity forward into uncharted territories.

Sustainability

Sustainability is about meeting our present needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet theirs, ensuring a balanced planet for all.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is the backbone of a thriving society, providing the essential systems,structures that support our daily lives and drive economic growth.

Empowerment

Empowerment is about giving individuals the tools, confidence, and opportunities to take control of their own lives and make impactful decisions.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Let us dream of a world where poverty is a thing of the past, where every individual has the opportunity to unleash their full potential. Together, we can make this dream a reality. By empowering the poorest of the poor, we can create a world where everyone has the chance to thrive and contribute to society.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
